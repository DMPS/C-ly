Class: ManagedStream

orca. ManagedStream

ManagedStream objects are obtained by calling the orca.createManagedStream method or handling the onAddStream event of a orca.Call (orca.Call#event:onAddStream). They are also created implicitly when passing a raw RTCMediaStream to the orca.Call#addStream method.
